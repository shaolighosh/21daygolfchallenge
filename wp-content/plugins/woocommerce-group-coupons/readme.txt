== Description ==

WooCommerce Group Coupons helps you to offer exclusive and targeted discounts to your customers.

== Installation ==

1. Go to *Plugins > Add New > Upload* and choose the plugin's zip file, or extract the contents and copy the `woocommerce-group-coupons` folder to your site's `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.  
2. Enable the plugin from the *Plugins* menu in WordPress.
3. Go to *WooCommerce > Group Coupons* and adjust the settings as needed. For new or existing coupons review the new Groups tab under Coupon Data.
